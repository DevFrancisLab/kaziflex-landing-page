

# KaziFlex Landing Page

A modern, professional landing page for KaziFlex — the app that empowers domestic workers to log tasks, get verified, and build tamper-proof work history.

## Design System
- **Colors**: Soft blue (`#3B82F6`) primary, white (`#FFFFFF`) background, vibrant yellow (`#FACC15`) accents
- **Style**: Clean, spacious, trustworthy, and tech-forward yet friendly and approachable

## Page Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- KaziFlex logo/wordmark on the left
- Links: Features, How It Works, About
- "Get Started" CTA button (blue) on the right

### 2. Hero Section
- **Headline**: "Flex Your Work. Show Your Value."
- **Subtext**: "KaziFlex helps domestic workers log tasks, get verified, and build a proof-of-labor history."
- **Primary CTA**: "Get Started" button in blue with yellow hover accent
- **Device Mockups**: Two stylized device frames (desktop + mobile) showing:
  - Worker logging tasks & hours
  - Employer confirming tasks & payment
  - Blockchain proof hash displayed as tamper-proof record

### 3. Features Section (4 cards)
- **Log Tasks & Hours** — Simple task selection with automatic hours and value calculation (clock icon)
- **Employer Confirmation** — Employers review work, confirm completion and payment (checkmark/shield icon)
- **Tamper-Proof Proof** — Blockchain hash stored as a permanent, verifiable work record (lock/chain icon)
- **Work History** — Chronological record of all verified work and earnings (calendar/list icon)

Each card will have an icon, title, and short description with clean card styling.

### 4. How It Works (3 steps)
- Step 1: Log your tasks and hours
- Step 2: Employer reviews and confirms
- Step 3: Get your tamper-proof work record
- Visual step indicators with icons and connecting lines

### 5. Task Icons Showcase
- Friendly illustrated icons for common domestic tasks: broom (cleaning), laundry, cooking, caregiving
- Gives the page a relatable, human feel

### 6. Call-to-Action Section
- **Headline**: "Start tracking your work today!"
- **CTA Button**: "Sign Up Now" (prominent blue button with yellow accent)
- Warm, inviting background gradient (soft blue)

### 7. Footer
- Links: About, Features, Contact
- Social media icons (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram)
- Copyright notice

## Technical Approach
- Single-page landing built as the Index page
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Lucide icons for feature cards and task illustrations
- CSS animations for subtle entrance effects on scroll
- No backend needed — purely a static landing page
